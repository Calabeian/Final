What is Tat-Hub?
Tat-Hub is a place for all tattoo enthusiasts to come together and discuss the stories
behind tattoos they already have, ideas of tattoos they want, advice for getting tattoos
and find tattoo shops near them.
Stories
The stories page is for users to post pictures of tattoos they have and explain the
often times deep backstories behind them.
Ideas
The ideas page is where users post either pictures or ideas of tattoos they want and
explain why they want it and what it means to them.
Advice
The advice page is where users can go for advice on choosing a tattoo, what to do
while getting a tattoo, and aftercare for their tattoos.
Shops
The shops page is going to contain a few of the top tattoo shops in each state.
