Before
When coming up with ideas for tattoos I believe that the most important part is that it matters to you. As long as the idea has meaning to you, odds are, you won't regret it.
Once you have your idea you need to find a shop. You want to find a tattoo artist who is good at the style you want and whether or not they take walk-ins or appointments only. After that you're set to get your tattoo!
During
I'm not gonna sugarcoat it, if you have never gotten a tattoo it is gonna hurt. You don't want to squirm alot in the chair because that makes the artist's job way harder.
They want you to be satisfied with your tattoo, but they really don't like it when you micro-manage. If you are courteous and listen to your artist everything will go smoothly and you will end up with a nice tattoo.
After
Once the artist has finished your tattoo don't forget to tip!
To keep your tattoo clean and healthy you are going to want to wash it everyday, for at least a week, with anti-bacterial soap and then lotion it with unscented lotion.
